About me

Born in the 4th of September 1999 in Eldoret the 'the city of champions' I was born. For my primary education I went to Testimony school which was quite near to where we were leaving at the time. I passed my Kenya Certificate of Primary Education with a total of 404 marks.I was called to join AIC chebisaas but my dad opted for Sunshine since we had moved to Nairobi. At Sunshine school is where I did my Kenya certificate of Secondary Education where I passed with B.

Immediately after high school, I joined Strathmore School for short course in accounting before proceeding to do a degree in Actuarial Science.

I have always had a passion for programming and developing software which I really wanted to pursue. After I told my dad this he looked for a school well capable of fulfilling my ambitions which are to get a well paying job and do something that I love and Moringa School was the place he found.
